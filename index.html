Project Code: SR26

We are SUN Racing, building Sunderland's revived Formula Student-style race car.

SUN Racing is a student motorsport team at the University of Sunderland, designing, building, and testing a single-seater racing car for the IMechE Formula Student competition. We welcome students from all backgrounds to develop engineering, management, and teamwork skills.
